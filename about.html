A view into Soleil White's mind

Hi! My name is Soleil White, I am a 18 year old artist that is achieving her artistic dreams. I was originally born in Ohio, but I am based out of Milwaukee, Wisconsin when I am not in school. Being an artist has been my dream since I was a little girl. I am proud that I have gotten myself this far and I am only going to go further!

I am currently a student at Cleveland Institue of Art where I am majoring in Sculpture and Expanded Media. With my major I want to create Wearable Sculpture's and hopefully one day I will have my own studio/fashion line that is different and more unique than normal clothing. I would also like to dress stars for the Met Gala! You can contact me at any of my socials below.
